MYZE


Myze was a project I worked on between 2016-2018 that I never really finished. It was more of a "Design the character first and come up with a game later" so it kinda mutated as it went along and had many different versions.



Myze was originally going to have a crown on her head to have sharpen her appearance, but the crownless spritework looked better (and more expressive) so I stopped drawing her with it after a while. Eventually it became a powerup that you would get in the game to boost some of your power and defense.



Some of the locations in the game. Sprites are either custom, or edited from public domain assets.
